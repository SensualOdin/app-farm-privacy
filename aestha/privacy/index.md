# Privacy Policy for Aestha

_Last updated: 2026-05-20_

Aestha ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we do and don't do with your data.

## TL;DR

- **We do not collect any data.**
- **We do not have a server.** Everything runs on your device.
- **We do not use third-party analytics, ads, trackers, or SDKs.**
- **We do not have user accounts** — there is nothing for us to lose.

## Information we collect

None. The app does not send any information about you off your device.

Everything you enter — including any text, settings, or content — is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults / SwiftData APIs. It never leaves your device. When you delete the app, that data is deleted with it.

## Information we share

None. We have nothing to share. We do not sell, rent, or transfer any data to anyone, because we do not have any.

## Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics, crash reporters, or ad networks.

## Children's privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is safe for users of any age.

## Changes to this policy

If the app ever changes to collect data, this policy will be updated and the app's release notes will say so. Until you see that, this policy stands as written.
