# Aestha Support

## Quick answers

**Where is my data stored?**
On your device. The app never sends data anywhere.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes — there is no network requirement.

**I deleted the app and lost my data.**
Data is stored inside the app's sandbox on your device. Deleting the app removes that sandbox. There is no cloud backup.

**The app crashed.**
Please email the address below with: your iOS version, your iPhone model, and what you were doing when it crashed.
